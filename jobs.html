Jobs

Lebenslauf Creator (Europass)

A Lebenslauf is a 2–3 page summary of a person's career and qualifications. Lebenslaufs are separated into different sections, with some common ones being contact information, academic history, professional experience, relevant skills, and awards.

Cover Letter Creator (Europass)

A cover letter is an introduction to yourself and your skills. Cover letters are not mandatory but recommended for most jobs. Every cover letter should contain an introduction, your relevant skills and experience, and why you would be a good fit for the job. Cover letters should conclude with a call to action for the job, such as asking to set up an interview. Click the above link for a free cover letter creator.

Job Search Websites

Those who have applied for or received a residence permit are allowed to work in Germany. Links to job boards include:

  • de.indeed.com/jobs
  • jobs4refugees.org
  • workeer.de
  • hr-integrate.com
  • arbeitsagentur.de

All German employees have rights while working in the country. Information about these rights and resources to resolve labor-related issues can be found on w2eu.info
