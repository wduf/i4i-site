Community Information

Discord Community Forum (NEEDS DICT)

English, German, Persian (ADD DICT)

Immigration4Iran's community forum where refugees and asylum seekers can go to ask for help with any step of the asylum-seeking process.

The Official Website of Berlin

Available in: Deutsch, English, Italiano, Français

The official website for the city of Berlin displays events in many languages to encourage the local community to go out and meet people. They display local monthly events like arts and crafts, opera, and various concerts.

Fare Network Football Map

Available in: English

Fare Networks is a football network that believes sports play an important role in community building. Their website linked below displays a helpful map of teams and football clubs that facilitate welcoming refugees to training sessions or other fun activities.
